Creating an onboarding link for your customer takes just a few minutes. Once created, you can send it to them and they can start sharing their data immediately.

## Step 1: Create a Company

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/%23company.png" alt="Companies dashboard" />
</Frame>

Go to [vern.so/companies](https://vern.so/companies) and click "Create Company" or "New Company." Enter your customer's name or company name. This creates a new company record where all their data will be organized.

## Step 2: Get Your Link

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/%23link.png" alt="Links interface" />
</Frame>

As soon as you create the company, Vern automatically generates a unique link for that customer. You'll see the link displayed in your company dashboard. You can copy it with one click.

## Step 3: Share the Link

Send the link to your customer through your preferred channelâ€”email, Slack, your platform, or however you normally communicate with them. They can access it immediately and start sharing their data.

## What Your Customer Can Do

Once your customer has the link, they can:
- Upload CSV files and spreadsheets
- Fill out forms you've created
- Authenticate Scouts to extract data from their existing systems

All their submissions automatically flow into your company dashboard, so you can see everything in one place.

## Next Steps

After your customer starts submitting data, you can:
- [Validate their data](/help-center/quickstart/validating-your-customers-data) using Templates
- [Clean messy data](/help-center/quickstart/cleaning-your-customers-messy-data) using Chat
- [Extract additional data](/help-center/quickstart/extracting-a-file-or-export) from their old systems using Scouts

## Security Tip

You can refresh the link at any time if you need to revoke access or generate a new one. The old link will stop working, keeping your customer's data secure.
